Zebra Finch

The Zebra Finch is a species of Australian birds that make excellent pets.

  • Scientific Name: Taeniopygia guttata
  • Average Length: 3.9 - 4.3 inches
  • Lifespan: 2 - 5 years
  • Habitat: Dry wooded grasslands

Zebra finches are loud and cheerful singers. Their calls can be a loud beep, meep, or oi. Each male's song is different, although birds of the same bloodline exhibit similarities.
